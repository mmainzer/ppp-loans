Explore Georgia's WARN Logs

Georgia's Department of Labor publicizes a list of business layoffs and closures on their site. The informations is reported by individual businesses to GDOL as required by the Federal Worker Adjustment and Retraining Notification Act (WARN). The list is not exhaustive, but a good indicator of where businesses are experiencing layoffs and possibly closures, and when they're experiencing them.

The data contains location information, which we've made available using the tool below. You can search by county, metro area, community development region, or by searching a drive time or radius. In the future, we'll make it possible to filter by data and industry as well.
